Waikoloa Beach Resort Golf

Hawaii's Premier 27-Hole Golf Experience

Choose any 9-hole courses alone, in any combination, or all together. The golf shop has everything needed for your game: apparel, accessories and rental clubs. Pick up items with the Waikoloa Beach Resort logo to remember your trip. This beautiful course offers spectacular views and unexpected challenges.

Beach Nine

Don't let the large greens fool you, these holes can be some of the toughest on the island. A par 5 has a Pacific view sure to distract you, with black lava flows in contrast to the blue ocean waters.

Lakes Nine

More than half the holes on this course contain water hazzards. Make strategic shots to bypass water and lava rock traps.

Kings' Nine

Views of Mauna Kea over this course's wide fairways are great for photos. One par 4 has a green reachable from the tees, but players risk ending up in a large sand bunker or behind lava boulders.

Practice

Fifteen acres of practice area exists to help you hone your driving, chipping and putting skills.

Pricing and Other Information

Sunrise Hale guests are entitled to special Kings' Club Membership golf rates, with big savings of up to 70% off regular rates.

Rates

  • 18-Hole Round Before 11am: $80
  • 18-Hole Round After 11am: $60
  • Golf club rentals: $25

Dress Code
  • Proper golf attire is required for all players while using the course or practice facilities. All gentlemen must wear collared shirts, with slacks or golf shorts.
  • No tee shirts, tank tops, denim or swimsuits are permitted.
  • Dresses, skirts, slacks, mid-length golf shorts are considered appropriate attire for the ladies.
  • No halter tops, tee shirts, tank tops, denim, cut-offs or swimsuits are permitted.
  • Soft-spikes or spike-less shoes are required for both men and women.
Golf Clubs

In an effort to maintain a proper pace of play for the enjoyment of all golfers on the course; the course requires all golfers to have their own set of clubs. Each set must have a minimum of eight clubs to be considered a set. If you are traveling to Hawaii with multiple sets in a single bag, they offer complimentary day bags.
